英语议论类作文

注：每组句型按背诵难度从易到难排列。

文首引题

格式：(开头+论题+)重要性(+对我有影响+具体影响)

开头

「讨论」开头

  • We've had a disscussion about …
  • Today I am going to talk something about …

「近来」开头

  • Recently, …
  • Nowadays, …

「迅速发展」开头

  • With the rapid development of …
  • With the rapid increasing numbers of …

「总所周知」开头

  • We all know that …
  • As we all know, …
  • As is known to all, …
  • It is known to all that …

论题

不良现象

  • … has become a problem we have to face.
  • … are growing more and more serious.

中立现象

  • … has been brought into focus.
  • … has been a hot topic among people.
  • … has been playing an increasingly important role in our day-to-day life.

重要性

  • … plays an important role / part in our daily life.
  • It is necessary / important / proper / helpful for … to …
  • … is an important way of …
  • As the say goes "…"

对我有影响

  • It has a deep influcece on …
  • It has a significant effect on …
  • It makes a significant difference on …
  • It affects me a lot.

提示

  • 「开头+论题」一般是必备项。若文章已给出则无需再写。
  • 不良现象一般不需要重要性和对我的影响。

陈述事件现象

格式：以某人做例子+引入时间+事件+结果

以某人做例子

  • Take … as an example.

结果

  • As a result, …
  • It made / left a … impression on me.

提示

  • 不能仅仅陈述一个事件，需要有足够的铺垫和细节。

    例1：写「塞车」，第二句写出了塞车的原因和结果，因此更好。

    1. There was a traffic jam on my way to school.
    2. A car accident happened on my way to school, and the traffic was so heavy that I got stuck in it. I could hardly get out of it.

    例2：写「受伤」，第二句写出了原因，描绘了情景，因此更好。

    1. I hurt myself, a man came to help me.
    2. On my way to school, I fell off my bike and hurt my leg. Just then a kind man came over to help me and called my parents.

  • 注意使用一般过去时进行陈述。

补充：有关批判的短语

  • 视而不见：sb. turn a blind eye to sth.
  • 置若罔闻：sb. turn a deaf ear to sth.

补充：「然而」连接词

  • However, …
  • Nevertheless, …

补充：其它连接词

  • Thus, …
  • Therefore, …
  • After all, …

观点

格式：用「结构」写总起+优点+过渡+缺点(+「在我看来」+自己观点)

结构

递进式

有无后缀 -ly 都一样，但注意如果有后缀则用于结构的序数词都要有后缀。

  • First, … Second, … Third, …
  • Firstly, … Secondly, … Thirdly, …

并列式

格式：首先+多个另外补充+最后

  • First of all, …
  • What's more, …
  • In addition, …
  • Moreover, …
  • At last, …
  • Last but not least, …

总起

  • Different people have different opinions.
  • Different people hold different attitudes / opinions towards…
  • People's views on … vary from person to person. Some think that … However, other believe that …

优缺点原因

  • Because …
  • The reason is that …
  • The reasons are as follow.

过渡

  • 详见关于「然而」连接词的补充。
  • However, every / each coin has two sides.
  • … has both advantages and disadvantages.
  • It has brought us a lot of benefits, but has caused some serious problems as well.

「在我看来」

  • In my view / opinion, …
  • From my point of view, …
  • As far as I'm concerned, …

自己观点

  • I think (that) …
  • I agree that …
  • I don't agree / disagree that …
  • It is wrong / right to …

做法

格式：过渡+用「结构」写多个做法

「结构」详见「重要性」一节。

过渡

  • Here are some suggestions for it.
  • What can we do for it? Here is some advice.
  • We should take action / steps / measures to deal with the problems.
  • It is necessary for … to deal with … properly.

做法

  • It is good for us to …
  • We should … when we …
  • I think (that) … , because it is a good way to …
  • We are supposed to …
  • We ought to do …

提示

  • 注意人称的单一性。祈使句多用于第二人称，因此用祈使句讲做法时不要与第一人称混用。讲做法建议使用第一人称。
  • 每个做法建议用不同句型。
  • 在提出做法前，可以承接相关谚语。
  • 适当写出不这么做的后果：Otherwise, …

文末升华

格式：「总而言之」+做法+呼吁

「总而言之」

  • In a word, …
  • All in all, …
  • Generally speaking, …
  • In short, …

呼吁

  • Let's learn to …
  • Let's try out best to do an act of …
  • Let's do whatever we can do to …
  • If everyone makes a tiny contribution, the world will be …
  • If more people … , there will be…

[短语] 补充：传承、弘扬

  • pass on sth. to the younger generation
  • pass down … to sb.
  • spread … to the world

补充：名言

实践

  • Practice makes perfect. 熟能生巧。
  • Experience is the mother of wisdom. 实践出真知。
  • Knowledge starts with practice. 实践出真知。

努力

  • No pains, no gains. 不劳无获。
  • Where there is a will, there is a way. 有志者事竟成。
  • Action speaks louder than words. 事实胜于雄辩。
  • No cross, no crown. 不经历风雨，怎么见彩虹。
  • Nothing is impossible to the man who will try. 世上无难事,只怕有心人。

学习

  • It's never too old to learn. 活到老，学到老。
  • There is no end to learning. 学无止境。

坚持

  • Rome was not built in a day. 冰冻三尺，非一日之寒。
  • Many drops makes a shower. 积少成多。

行动

  • Doing is better than saying. 行胜于言。
  • A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step. 千里之行始于足下。
  • A good beginning is half done. 良好的开端是成功的一半。

其他

  • Do as Romans do in Rome. 入乡随俗。

  • A friend in need is a friend indeed. 患难见真情。（朋友）

  • Failure is the mother of success. 失败是成功之母。（成功与失败）

  • All work and no play make Jack a dull boy. 只工作不玩耍，聪明孩子也变傻。（劳逸结合）

  • Different attitudes determine different altitude.

    态度决定高度。（态度）
